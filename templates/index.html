Who we are?

We are a community of over 3500 active and engaging African youths ambitiously building a career across different fields.
Upfolio is a youth development organisation passionate about the personal and professional development of African youths. We do this through democratising opportunities for youths and creating authentic youth development projects and programs.

What makes us unique?

We've realised that opportunities are much more than job postings especially for the African youth demographic always looking to measure up with the best talents in the world. Much more than job opportunities, we also provide you with internships, volunteering, fellowships, scholarships, competitions, fully funded programs and grant opportunities.

Learn More

What do we offer?


  • A community of over 3500 active and engaging African youths ambitiously building a career in different work niches.


  • A frequently updated job board to help you find niche-specific job opportunities.


  • Programs and projects to equip you for the next opportunity.


  • A bi-monthly newsletter filled with career tips and round up of opportunities.
