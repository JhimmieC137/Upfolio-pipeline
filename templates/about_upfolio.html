About Upfolio

Upfolio is an award winning youth development organisation built with the goal of helping Young Africans go global. We are a community based organization, democratizing opportunities for youths and providing an environment to train members of the community on how to effectively take charge of these opportunities. Overall, we are passionate about young people and are set out to help young people become the best versions of themselves.


We have a vision to help young Africans find their place on the global stage by providing opportunities that empowers them and secures them for the future. Since January 2020, we have grown into a community of above 3500 active and engaging youths and in the past two years, we have successfully enabled over 100 young people win various opportunities via our projects and capacity development programs.


Our Team

Upfolio has an active volunteer workforce that comprises of young people from different backgrounds, locations and ethnicity, dedicated towards making the cooperate dream a reality. Each volunteer functions within different teams headed by selected team leads, contributing energy, time, skills and ideas towards the roles they play, set goals and targets. As such, we have developed a commited team of continually evolving individuals working towards making a positive change in the lives of young people.
